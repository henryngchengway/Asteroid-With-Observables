Asteroids


Press [w] to move to the pointed direction
Press [arrow left] key to rotate the ship to the left
Press [arrow right] key to rotate the ship to the right
Press [space] key to shoot bullets

Turn up the volume for sound effects!!
Reminder: Dont panic when the game shows up gameOver and there is no collision with your ship
This is not a bug because the asteroid spawning is in random x & y axis. Therefore, there is a chance that
the asteroid spawn beside your ship and it immediately detects that there is a collision. But nevertheless,
the chance of getting this is quite low if you keep moving your ship. Have fun!!
